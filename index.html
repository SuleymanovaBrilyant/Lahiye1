CHAMPIONS LEAGUE

If we mentioned above, let's give a brief explanation that it is the most famous football tournament in Europe, where the clubs that are successful in their national championships compete. According to the rules, the teams that pass the play-off stage and fall directly into the group competitions are divided into groups. CONFERENCE LEAGUE

In the final match, "Roma" defeated "Feyenoord" with a score of 1–0 and became the winner of the first Conference League tournament.
